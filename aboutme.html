    About Me

    Greetings! My name is Xinge Zhu. I obtained my PhD degree at Multimedia lab in CUHK. My supervisor is Prof. Dahua Lin.

    My research lies at computer vision and deep learning, particularly in Scene Understanding and Perception, Point-cloud 3D Detection and Segmentation, etc.

    During my PhD, I spent wonderful time at SenseTime, Baidu and Inceptio. I also work closely with Prof. Yuexin Ma, Prof. Ruigang Yang, Dr. Jianping Shi and Prof. Dinesh Manocha.
